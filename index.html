Sailor Moon

1992-1993

  • Communicator

    The Communicators were special devices used by the Sailor Guardians to keep in touch with each other.

  • Weapons

    Sailor Moon uses a wide variety of weapons. The most used ones are magical rods, sticks and wands.

  • Tiara

    Tiaras are part of any Sailor Guardian uniform, except Sailor Cosmos, Sailor Galaxia, and Eternal Sailor Moon. Sailor Moon's tiara served as her first weapon. Sailor Moon's first tiara was given to her when she first transformed into her regular Sailor form as Sailor Moon.

  • Odango Covers

    The Red Hair Pieces appears in Sailor Moon's hair when she transforms, decorating her odango. They have the ability to create supersonic waves.

  • Transformation Items

    To face the forces of evil and protect the world from darkness, Usagi Tsukino uses several items to transform. Shouting a certain phrase while holding the item makes her appearance change, gain items she can use to fight off evil and gives her magical powers. Each season of the series has a different item she uses to transform.

  • Disguise Pen

    The Disguise Pen is a pen that was given to Usagi by Luna, so she could disguise herself in order to complete a mission more easily. While she looks like the person she wants to be, she also apparently gains the knowledge of whoever she changes into.

  • Powerful Objects

    Throughout the series Usagi collects several extremely powerful objects that give her new, stronger magical powers.Those objects come with a cost, often consuming all of Usagi's energy upon use.
